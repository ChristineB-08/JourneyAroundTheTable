Our handmade pasta

Welcome to our Italian Fresh Pasta Workshop, where we bring the heart of Italy to your kitchen. In this immersive experience, you'll learn the ancient craft of making authentic, handmade pasta from scratch, using only the finest Italian ingredients.
At the core of our workshop is a commitment to quality. We believe that the best pasta starts with the best ingredients.
That’s why we use exclusively high-quality Italian flour, eggs, and artisanal products sourced directly from local farms and producers across Italy. Each ingredient is carefully selected for its superior flavor and authenticity, ensuring that every bite of pasta carries the true essence of Italian cuisine.
From the soft, velvety texture of the dough to the perfect al dente bite, our pasta is made with the utmost care and devotion.

Our Types of Fresh Pasta

  • Tortellini

    Delicious Italian stuffed pasta, typically filled with meat or cheese.

  • Tortelli

    Soft, stuffed pasta with a variety of savory fillings.

  • Colored Tortelli

    Vibrantly colored stuffed pasta for a visual and tasty treat.

  • Pasta

    Classic pasta shapes, perfect for any dish.

Tortellini

  • Tortellini alla Panna

    Traditional tortellini stuffed with a mixture of pork, prosciutto, and Parmesan cheese, served in a rich cream sauce with a hint of nutmeg.

  • Tortellini in Brodo

    Delicate tortellini filled with veal and cheese, served in a flavorful homemade chicken broth for a comforting classic.

Tortelli

  • Tortelli di Zucca

    A classic from Mantova: fresh tortelli filled with roasted pumpkin, Parmesan cheese, and a touch of nutmeg, served with brown butter and crispy sage.

  • Tortelli al Ragù di Cinghiale

    Handmade tortelli filled with wild boar and herbs, served in a hearty and rich wild boar ragù sauce.

Colored Tortelli

  • Tortelli di Spinaci e Ricotta

    Vibrant green tortelli made from spinach dough, filled with creamy ricotta cheese, and served with a light tomato and basil sauce.

  • Tortelli di Carota e Mascarpone

    Tortelli filled with sweet carrot and mascarpone, paired with a tangy lemon butter sauce and toasted pine nuts.

  • Tortelli di Barbabietola e Gorgonzola in three colors

    Rich purple beetroot tortelli filled with tangy Gorgonzola cheese and walnuts, served in a buttery, sage-infused sauce.

Traditional & Modern Pasta

  • Tagliatelle al Ragù alla Bolognese

    Classic ribbon-like tagliatelle pasta served with a slow-cooked, hearty ragù made from beef, pork, and tomatoes. A timeless Emilia-Romagna specialty.

  • Spaghetti alla Carbonara

    A modern twist on the Roman classic, with al dente spaghetti tossed in a creamy sauce made from eggs, Pecorino Romano, guanciale, and black pepper.

  • Fettuccine al Limone e Tartufo

    Fresh fettuccine pasta tossed in a creamy lemon sauce with a drizzle of aromatic truffle oil, garnished with shaved black truffles for a luxurious touch.
